About Us

Two girls, a sunday afternoon and a lot of time in hand. Now, that means some serious fashion in the making, doesn't it? How can it even be anything else! Especially when they're bored out of their wits with their respective high-profile PR jobs. Yup,that's us, Urmi Daga and Hemal Ved. So we did the inevitable and gave in to that ultimate seduction. Fashion. It's the dope we yearn for and the money we always blew up for...

But it just doesn't end here. It took us one and half year, as stylists, to work with some of the finest people in the industry; Having assisted for magazines like Filmfare, FHM, People and having shot with celebrities like Sonakshi Sinha, Kunal Roy Kapoor, Shraddha Kapoor, Prateik Babbar, Shahzahn Padamsee, Milan Lutharia, Sonu Sood, Eesha Koppikhar etc we are now ready to enter the blogging zone. But we're not here to mother you or to burden you with gazillion different pieces of advice on hardcore style and fashion. You know your stuff better than we do! But if it's fine tuning you're looking for, you've come on the right page.

Non-fiction shows

WWH started off with styling anchors like Karan Singh Grover, Gaurav Kapoor, Ranvijay, Vj Bani, Pravesh Rana, Raghu and Rajiv for Roadies, Vj Aliyah, Natasha Suri for non-fiction shows on GEC channels namely, UTV, MTV and Channel V. Styling singers and music producers like Amit Trivedi, Clinton, Karsh Kale, Vishal Dadlani etc for Coke Studio @ MTV.

Advertisements,Promos,Photoshoots

Shifting on to something that we enjoy doing the most, some more work by WWH is as follows: Birla Sunlife, Parker, Mc Donalds, Ultratech, Gadget Guru, Kelloggs, Movers & Shakers, Indian Idol, KFC, Alto, Pizza Hut, Kara, Honda, JK Cement, Lookbook shoot for Pepperfry.com, Photoshoots with photographers like Ram Bherwani, Munna S, Suresh Natrajan, Gaurav Sawn, Rohan Shreshtha, Taras Taraporvala, Avinash Gowariker etc.

Personal Shopping

You can completely imagine having your own personal shopper. WWH helps you research the shops for you, based on your style, budget, and lifestyle, understand and learn about your body shape and the styles that would suit you. We make you simplify your everyday dressing by helping you cleanse your wardrobe, add on must-haves and subtract unsuitable items, making space for some new styles.

So now it doesn't take rocket science to figure what's on our agenda, does it? We breathe anything that's everything under the fashion umbrella, even if that means the business of fashion. So this is where the 'F' word is no taboo, where a 'size zero' shouldn't intimidate you and where hi-end luxurious brands are not recommended, just like your fashion magazines do, and rip your pockets apart.
